IMPACT TIMELINE

Our Journey of Milestones

From a small online initiative to a NGO delivering real community impact — every step has mattered.

Founded2021
Foundation

Wise Up is Born

Two students begin posting motivational and inspirational quotes on Facebook and Telegram, planting the seed of what would become a NGO.

🌱 Beginning of a movement
Growth2023
Online Growth

Multi-Platform Expansion

Expanded to Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and LinkedIn, building an engaged online audience that resonates with our message of empowerment and positive change.

📱 Thousands of followers across platforms
Oct2024
Girl Child · Partnership

International Day of the Girl Child

First major on-ground program in partnership with Lite Art Foundation. Visited a local school, delivered empowerment sessions, and provided sanitary pads to over 100 girls.

👷🏾 100+ girls reached
Mar2025
Education

Empower Young Minds 1.0 — Maiden Edition

Our first standalone education outreach program. Visited a primary school, provided stationery, and shared inspiring stories encouraging students to stay in school and dream big.

📚 Students equipped and inspired
May2025
Community · Partnership

Children’s Day Celebration

Partnered with Lite Art Foundation to celebrate Children’s Day, bringing joy, gifts, and memorable experiences to children in our community.

🎉 Community joy and celebration
Mar2025
Community

WhatsApp Community Launched

Launched our always-on WhatsApp community, now hosting 100+ members with monthly discussions on personal development, gender equality, career growth, and social issues.

💬 100+ active community members
Jun2025
Health · Partnership

Drug Awareness Advocacy Campaign

4-day collaboration with The Drug Free Project to advocate against substance abuse across the community. Shared resources and insights to help individuals make informed, healthy choices.

🛡️ 4-day community advocacy
Oct2025
Girl Child

International Day of the Girl Child 2025

Second annual Girl Child Day program — expanding our reach and deepening our impact with more girls receiving resources, empowerment sessions, and mentorship.

✨ Annual initiative growing stronger
Mar2026
Education · Skills

Empower 2.0 & Skill Acquisition Program

Our most ambitious programs yet — Empower Young Minds 2.0 expanded our school outreach, while our brand-new Skill Acquisition Program taught youth web development, video editing, content creation, and digital marketing.

🚀 Biggest programs to date
