OUR HISTORY

From a Small Online Initiative
to an NGO

2021

The Beginning

Wise Up was founded as a small online initiative by two students — posting inspirational and motivational quotes on Facebook and Telegram with a passion for making a positive impact.

2023

Growing Online Presence

Expanded to Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, LinkedIn, and other platforms — building an engaged community of followers who resonated with our message of empowerment and positive change.

2024

Becoming an NGO

Evolved into Wise Up Foundation a non-governmental organization. The team grew from two to nine members and began running real on-ground programs and partnerships.

2025–26

Expanding Reach & Impact

Team grew to 40+ members. Established a university chapter (LAUTECH). Launched WhatsApp community (100+ members), and delivered 7+ major programs across Nigeria.

UN SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS

Our Work is Rooted in the SDGs

Everything we do is intentionally designed to contribute to three of the United Nations' Global Goals — because local action drives global change.

4 Quality Education

Education as the Key to Opportunity

We believe that education unlocks potential. Through school visits, stationery drives, and our Empower Young Minds program, we promote the importance of staying in school and pursuing knowledge.

8 Decent Work & Economic Growth

Empowering Youth for the Economy

Our Skill Acquisition Program and career mentorship initiatives give young people in-demand digital and creative skills — directly addressing youth unemployment and economic exclusion.

10 Reduced Inequalities

Equity for All — Women and Men

We take a balanced approach to empowerment, championing gender equality while also recognizing positive masculinity — creating a more inclusive society where everyone has access to opportunities.
