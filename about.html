OUR STORY

Born from Passion,
Driven by Purpose

What started as two students posting motivational quotes online in 2021 has grown into a Foundation with 40+ team members, a university chapter, and real impact across Nigerian communities.

WISE UP FOUNDATION

Inspiring Positive Change
Since 2021

Wise Up Foundation is a student-led NGO dedicated to making a positive impact in our community. We’re passionate about empowering individuals, promoting education, and advocating for a better future and a better world.

We align our work with the United Nations’ SDGs — Goal 4 (Quality Education), Goal 8 (Decent Work & Economic Growth), and Goal 10 (Reduced Inequalities).

📅 Founded 2021 · NGO 2024
🎯
Our Mission

Empowering Communities Through Action

To empower individuals, promote education, and advocate for a better future — raising awareness, supporting vulnerable populations, and fostering career development.

🌍
Our Vision

A World Aligned with the SDGs

A world where quality education is accessible to all, decent work opportunities exist for every young person, and inequalities are systematically reduced — for both women and men.

✨
Our Tagline
“...inspiring positive change”

Every initiative, every conversation, every program we run is anchored in this simple but powerful belief.

OUR HISTORY

From a Small Online Initiative
to a NGO

2021

The Beginning

Founded as a small online initiative by two students — posting inspirational quotes on Facebook and Telegram with a passion for positive impact.

2023

Growing Online Presence

Expanded to Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, LinkedIn — building an engaged community resonating with our message of empowerment.

2024

Officially Becoming an NGO

Evolved into Wise Up Foundation. Team grew from two to nine members and began running real on-ground programs and partnerships.

2025–26

Expanding Reach & Impact

Team grew to 40+ Team members. Established a university chapter. Launched WhatsApp community (100+ members) and delivered 7+ major programs.

UN SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS

Our Work is Rooted in the SDGs

Everything we do is intentionally designed to contribute to three of the United Nations’ Global Goals.

4Quality Education

Education as the Key to Opportunity

Through school visits, stationery drives, and our Empower Young Minds program, we promote the importance of staying in school.

8Decent Work & Economic Growth

Empowering Youth for the Economy

Our Skill Acquisition Program and career mentorship give young people in-demand digital skills to build careers.

10Reduced Inequalities

Equity for All — Women and Men

We champion gender equality while also recognizing positive masculinity — creating a more inclusive society for everyone.
